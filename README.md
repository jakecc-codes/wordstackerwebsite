# Word Stacker
> by JakeCCz

Word Stacker is an online time capsule where you can share important words/pharses to mark an important historic event/memorial part in history.

The words/phrases that are entered are added to an ever-growning stack/tower of phrases entered by previous users of the website!
These messages are viewable by the public but **cannot be changed, replaced or removed**. If a word is removed at any point in time, the tower will crumble and fall at an instant, destroying all other words or phrases created in the process.
